title: 'Date Night with the LED Display'
---

Others stay up late watching movies, or playing games. I'd rather spend my time with you, my little companion.

I can't help but be drawn to your display of affection. I know time flies when we're together, but you always keep me in the loop.

Some might say I'm LED astray, but I think our relationship is timeless. The best part is you really know how to make every second count.
